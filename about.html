Todd Dearborn

“Translating data into stories that connect, engage, and convert.”

Todd Dearborn is a marketing strategist and storyteller based in Tampa, FL, with a Master of Science in Marketing from the McCombs School of Business at The University of Texas at Austin (’25). Over the past three years, he has built data-driven campaigns and user-centered digital experiences for a major tech firm, lawfirm, and many more for projects.

His signature approach marries advanced analytics (SQL, Python, R) with creative UX design—transforming complex financial products and case studies (like ETF deep-dives and mutual fund narratives) into intuitive visual stories that resonate with everyday investors.
